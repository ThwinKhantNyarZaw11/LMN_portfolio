Luxury Residence Interior Design

Project Overview

Led the complete interior design for a private luxury residence, blending modern aesthetics with timeless comfort. The project included space planning, material selection, lighting design, and furniture customization to create a sophisticated living environment.

Key Highlights

  • Curated premium materials such as natural stone, Italian marble, and walnut wood
  • Designed bespoke furniture and fixtures to match client lifestyle
  • Integrated layered lighting design with dimmable zones
  • Maximized natural light through layout orientation and mirror placement
  • Used smart home integration for lighting, temperature, and security

Tools & Techniques

  • AutoCAD and SketchUp for space planning and 3D modeling
  • V-Ray rendering for visual presentation
  • Adobe Photoshop and InDesign for concept boards
  • Site supervision and supplier coordination for flawless execution

Impact

  • Delivered a serene, functional, and elegant space praised by the client
  • Design featured in a local architecture & lifestyle magazine
  • Set a benchmark for future high-end residential projects
