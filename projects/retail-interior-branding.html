Retail Interior & Branding

Project Overview

Delivered a flagship retail interior for a lifestyle brand, aligning spatial design with visual identity. The goal was to create an immersive brand experience, improve shopper flow, and highlight key product collections through storytelling architecture.

Key Highlights

  • Translated brand values into material palettes and spatial language
  • Designed product display zones and focal walls to maximize engagement
  • Developed signage, lighting, and storefront in a cohesive style guide
  • Implemented customer journey mapping to optimize layout
  • Coordinated with graphic designers and merchandisers for cross-discipline harmony

Tools & Techniques

  • Retail layout planning in AutoCAD & Revit
  • Customer journey modeling with experience design software
  • Brand/space visual merge using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop

Impact

  • Increased customer dwell time and in-store conversion rates
  • Strengthened brand identity through spatial storytelling
  • Design adopted as a template for future store rollouts
