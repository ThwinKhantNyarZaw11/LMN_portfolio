Sustainable Material Showcase

Project Overview

Led the design and curation of a sustainable material showroom intended to inspire architects, designers, and developers to adopt eco-friendly materials in their projects. The exhibit focused on circular design principles, biodegradability, and low-carbon alternatives.

Key Highlights

  • Featured over 40 sustainable materials including bamboo composites, recycled glass tiles, and mycelium panels
  • Designed flexible display zones with modular shelving and QR-tagged samples
  • Included educational infographics and interactive touch screens
  • Collaborated with suppliers and researchers for accurate sourcing info
  • Used reclaimed wood and zero-VOC paints throughout exhibit space

Tools & Techniques

  • SketchUp and Enscape for environmental modeling
  • Material lifecycle research and green certifications database
  • Custom signage created using Illustrator & Canva

Impact

  • Influenced multiple local design firms to adopt sustainable alternatives
  • Exhibit received recognition from a regional green building council
  • Generated over 500+ material requests within 3 months of launch
